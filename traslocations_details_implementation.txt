### HOW TO IMPLEMENT TRAs

I divide TRAs in 3 sub-categories corresponding to
1. cut&paste TRAs : a genomic segment from chrX moves to chrY
2. copy&paste TRAs - interspersed DUPs : a genomic segment from chrX gets copied into chrY
3. reciprocal TRAs : a genomic region A on chrX and a genomic region B on chrY swap places

according to the VCF 4.2 specifications, all of these instances have to be represented with them BND class
